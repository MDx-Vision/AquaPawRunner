GoPAWZ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GoPAWZ, operated by Verdant Imperium LLC. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, and mobile canine fitness services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the best care for your pets, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address (for service delivery).
  • Pet Information: Dog name, breed, age, weight, medical history, vaccination records, and behavioral notes.
  • Emergency Contact Information: Name and phone number of your designated emergency contact and veterinarian.
  • Session Media: Photos and videos captured during fitness sessions for your records and, with consent, marketing purposes.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe; we do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, device type, browser information, and app/website interaction logs.
  • Referral Data: If you participate in our referral program, we track referral codes and successful referrals.
  • Communications: Records of your communications with us, including support requests and feedback.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate fitness session bookings and service delivery at your location.
  • Send service updates, booking confirmations, reminders, and session reports.
  • Share session photos and videos with you after completed sessions.
  • Process payments and issue refunds when applicable.
  • Administer our referral program and apply rewards.
  • Improve app functionality, user experience, and service quality.
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. SMS and Email Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages including booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and session completion notifications. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of non-essential SMS by replying STOP. You may opt out of marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email.

5. Session Recordings

GoPAWZ may record photos and videos during fitness sessions. These recordings are used to:

  • Provide you with visual updates of your dog's session.
  • Document session activities for quality and safety purposes.
  • With your consent, use for marketing and promotional materials.

You may opt out of marketing use by contacting us at admin@go-pawz.com. Session recordings are stored securely and retained for 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Service Delivery: GoPAWZ staff assigned to your booking to provide services.
  • Third-Party Service Providers:
    • Neon (database hosting)
    • Stripe (payment processing)
    • Resend (email communications)
    • Twilio (SMS notifications)
    • Replit (application hosting)
  • Legal Necessity: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our community.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure socket layer (SSL) technology, and password hashing to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account closure, we retain data for up to 3 years for legal and business purposes, or as required by law. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. To exercise any rights, contact us at admin@go-pawz.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we learn that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

11. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and remember preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
